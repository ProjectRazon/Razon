>[!DEFINITION] Definition: Abelian Group
>
>A [[index|group]] $(G, \cdot)$ is called **abelian**, if its operation is commutative:
>
>$$
>a \cdot b = b \cdot a \qquad \forall a,b \in G
>$$
>
