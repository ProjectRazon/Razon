>[!DEFINITION] Definition: Binary Operation
>
>A **binary operation** on a [[Sets|set]] $S$ is an [[Operation]] $f: S \times S \to S$.
>
>>[!NOTATION]-
>>
>>Instead of writing $f(a, b)$, we often write $a\, f\, b$.
>>
>
