>[!DEFINITION] Definition: Cyclic Polygon
>
>A [[Polygons|polygon]] is **cyclic** iff there exists a [[Circle]] in the [[Planes|plane]] of the polygon such that all [[Polygons|vertices]] of the polygon lie on the circle.
>
