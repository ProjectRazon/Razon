>[!DEFINITION] Definition: Dense Subset
>
>Let $(X, \tau)$ is a [[Topological Spaces|topological space]].
>
>A [[Sets|subset]] $S \subseteq X$ is **dense** iff its [[Closure]] is $X$.
>
>$$
>\overline{S} = X
>$$
