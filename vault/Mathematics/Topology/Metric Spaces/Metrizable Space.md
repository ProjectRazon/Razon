>[!DEFINITION] Definition: Metrizable Space
>
>A [[Topological Spaces|topological space]] $(X,\tau)$ is **metrizable** if there exists a [[index|metric]] $d$ on it, such that $\tau$ is the [[index|metric topology]] induced by $d$.
>
