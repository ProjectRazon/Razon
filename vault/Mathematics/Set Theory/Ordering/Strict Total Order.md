>[!DEFINITION] Definition: Strict Total Order
>
>A **strict total order** of a [[Set]] $A$ is a [[Strict Partial Order]] of $A$ in which any two elements are comparable:
>
>$$
>a \lt b \text{ or } b \lt a \qquad \forall a,b \in A
>$$
>
