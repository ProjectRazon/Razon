>[!DEFINITION] Definition: $n$-Tuple
>
>An $n$-**Tuple** $(a_1,\cdots,a_n)$ is a [[Sets|set]] of $n$ [[Ordered Pairs]]
>
>$$
>\{(1,a_1), \cdots, (n,a_n)\}
>$$
>
>^tuple
>

>[!THEOREM] Theorem: Equality of $n$-Tuples
>
>Two $n$-[[Tuples]] $(a_1,\cdots,a_n)$ and $(b_1,\cdots,b_n)$ are [[Sets#equality|equal]] if and only if $a_1 = b_1, \cdots, a_n = b_n$:
>
>$$
>(a_1,\cdots,a_n) = (b_1,\cdots,b_n) \iff a_1 = b_1 \land \cdots \land a_n = b_n
>$$
