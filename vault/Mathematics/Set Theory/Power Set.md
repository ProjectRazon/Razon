>[!DEFINITION] Definition: Power Set
>
>The **power set** of a [[Sets|set]] $M$ is the [[Collections|collection]] of all [[Sets|subsets]] of $S$.
>
>$$
>\{T \mid T \subseteq M\}
>$$
>
>>[!NOTATION]-
>>
>>$$
>>\mathscr{P}(M) \qquad \mathcal{P}(M) \qquad \mathsf{P}(M) \qquad P(M) \qquad \mathbb{P}(M) \qquad 2^M
>>$$
>>
>
